Regional Vision Zero Traffic Safety Concerns

DVRPC and its partners are developing a Regional Vision Zero (RVZ) plan to make roadways safer for all users. We want to hear your traffic safety concerns. Use this mapping tool to report a concern by selecting a location and answering a few questions.

  1. To begin, click the blue “Submit a Traffic Safety Concern” button (bottom left of map).
  2. Click on the map to add a point where your safety concern is located (e.g.: intersection, driveway, etc.).
  3. Answer the questions in the form.
  4. Click the blue “Submit” button to send us your traffic safety concern.
You can also vote for an existing comment by clicking the thumbs up emoji. Comments gathered here will help inform the RVZ plan to improve roadway safety.

DVRPC serves strictly as an advisory agency. Any planning or design concepts as prepared by DVRPC are conceptual and may require engineering design and feasibility analysis. Actual authority for carrying out any planning proposals rest solely with the governing bodies of the states, local governments or authorities that have the primary responsibility to own, manage or maintain any transportation facility.
