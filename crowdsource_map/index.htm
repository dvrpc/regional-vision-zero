The DVRPC and its partners are developing a Regional Vision Zero (RVZ) plan to make roadways safer for all modes, and want to hear from you about traffic safety concerns you have. This mapping tool allows users to select a location and answer a few questions about your identified traffic safety concern. Comments gathered here will help inform the RVZ plan to improve roadway safety.
